HOW IT WORKS

We do not pitch with slides. We build your actual site first.

Our sales call is simple: we visit your office, open your laptop, and show you a working website using your photos, products, and business name. If you like it, we launch. If not, you pay nothing.

01

We visit and build

We meet you at your shop or office in Kathmandu. We bring a draft built from your real content, not placeholder text. You see it live on your own device.

02

You approve on the spot

If you love it, you pay a single fee of Rs. 15,000, reduced from Rs. 25,000. If you do not, there is no charge and no awkward follow-up. Your call.

03

Handover and ownership

We create the GitHub repository under your own email, deploy on Vercel, and give you a simple editor to change photos, text, and pricing anytime. You own everything.
